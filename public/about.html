Our Story

Darpan was born from a simple idea: video conferencing should be seamless, accessible, and free from technical barriers. The Sanskrit word "Darpan" means mirror, reflecting our commitment to creating clear, lifelike connections between people.

Founded in 2023, our team of passionate engineers and designers set out to build a browser-based video conferencing platform that works effortlessly across devices without the need for downloads or complicated setup.

Today, Darpan is used by thousands of people worldwide for work meetings, online education, virtual events, and staying connected with loved ones.

rocket_launch Our Mission

To democratize video communication by providing a high-quality, accessible platform that works instantly for everyone, everywhere, without technical barriers or costs.

visibility Our Vision

A world where distance is no longer a barrier to meaningful human connection, where technology enhances rather than complicates our ability to collaborate and communicate.
