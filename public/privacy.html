Privacy Policy

At Darpan, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

1. Information We Collect

When using our video conferencing service:

  • Name and email address (optional, for meeting identification)
  • Meeting room codes and participation data
  • Audio and video streams (during meetings only)
  • Chat messages (during meetings only)
  • Technical information such as device type, browser version, and IP address

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve our video conferencing service
  • Enable communication between meeting participants
  • Troubleshoot technical issues
  • Ensure service security and prevent abuse
  • Send important service updates and notifications

3. Data Security

We protect your data through:

  • End-to-end encryption for all video and audio streams
  • Secure storage of any persistent data
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Strict access controls for our staff

4. Data Retention

We retain different types of data for different periods:

  • Meeting content (video, audio, chat): Not stored after meeting ends
  • Meeting metadata: 30 days
  • Account information: Until account deletion
  • Technical logs: 90 days

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request data deletion
  • Object to data processing
  • Export your data

To exercise these rights, please contact our support team through the Contact Support page.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies to:

  • Maintain your session
  • Remember your preferences
  • Ensure service security

We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics.

7. Contact Information

For privacy-related inquiries, please contact:

  • Email: privacy@darpan.com
  • Through our Contact Support page
