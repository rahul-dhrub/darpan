How Darpan Works

Follow these simple steps to join or create a meeting in seconds. No downloads or installations required.

1

Create a Meeting

Visit the Darpan homepage and click on "Create New Meeting" to generate a unique meeting room. Alternatively, you can create a meeting link to share with participants without joining immediately.

add_box

Pro Tip: Bookmark your frequently used meeting rooms for quick access.

2

Set Up Your Devices

Before joining, check your camera and microphone settings in the device preview page. You can select your preferred audio and video devices, test your microphone, and even set up background effects before entering the meeting.

settings

Pro Tip: Use background blur or images for privacy during meetings.

3

Invite Participants

Share your meeting link or room code with participants via email, messaging apps, or calendar invites. Anyone with the link can join instantly from their browser without creating an account.

share

Pro Tip: Copy the meeting link directly from the address bar during an active meeting.

4

Start Collaborating

Once in the meeting, use the controls at the bottom of the screen to toggle your microphone, camera, share your screen, raise your hand, send chat messages, and more. Pin important speakers or switch between different layout views as needed.

groups

Pro Tip: Use keyboard shortcuts like 'M' for mute/unmute and 'V' for toggling video.

5

End Meeting & Follow Up

When the meeting is complete, click "Leave" to exit. If you recorded the session, you'll have access to the recording for review or sharing with others who couldn't attend.

check_circle

Pro Tip: Rate your meeting experience to help us improve Darpan.
